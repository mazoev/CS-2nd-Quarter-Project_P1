Storyline:

The Trolls, known for their infectious happiness, embark on a journey to save their friends from the pessimistic Bergens, who believe happiness can only be attained by consuming Trolls.

Genre: Animation, Adventure, Comedy

Review

"Trolls" is a vibrant and visually appealing animated adventure. With catchy music and a positive message, it's an entertaining film for all ages. The voice cast, led by Anna Kendrick and Justin Timberlake, brings the characters to life, creating a whimsical world that captivates audiences. It's a colorful celebration of joy and friendship.
